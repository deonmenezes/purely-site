FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out anytime — we love hearing from you.

Is Purely free to use?+

Yes. Core scanning and ingredient analysis are free. Purely Plus unlocks personalized health goals, advanced insights, and weekly reports.

How accurate is the scoring?+

Our scoring combines ingredient quality, nutritional density, and processing level. It's reviewed by dietitians and updated as new research is published.

Does it work with any product?+

Purely supports millions of products across the US and is growing daily. If a product isn't found, you can submit it and we'll add it within 48 hours.

Is my data private?+

Absolutely. All scans are encrypted end-to-end and never sold. You can export or delete your data at any time.

Which devices are supported?+

Purely is available on iPhone today, with Android launching soon. Sign up on the waitlist to get notified.
